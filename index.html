About our club

Community & fun

Tonbridge Pickleball Club is all about bringing people together for a great time. We're a social, fun, and incredibly welcoming group for anyone who loves or wants to learn pickleball.

Our social evenings are packed with fun games and drills designed to help you meet new people, learn new skills, and most importantly, enjoy the game!

Play & progress

While we love our social sessions, we also offer competitive opportunities. We actively pursue matches with neighboring clubs and participate in competitions across the UK.

Whether you're looking to stay active, keep fit, or master the sport, our club provides a fantastic environment for both doubles and occasional singles play. We welcome individuals of all ages, sexes, and backgrounds.

Our sessions

Date:
Every Thursday evening

Time:
7:30 PM - 9:00 PM

Venue:
Weald of Kent Grammar School, Tudeley Lane, Tonbridge, TN9 2JP

Playing time:
We have access to three courts, allowing for up to 16 people. We normally play king of the court and rotate games every 12 mins.

Costs:
Joining the club is free! Members join our WhatsApp group (for keeping in touch), then register and pay upfront (around £5-6) on Spond for 1.5 hours of play.

Frequently asked questions

When do you play?

We play every Thursday evening from 7:30 PM to 9:00 PM.

Where do you play?

How many people play regularly?
