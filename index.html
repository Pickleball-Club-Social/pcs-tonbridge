We're a group of people that love Pickleball

Join our social Pickleball sessions every Thursday evening from 7:30 to 9:00pm at the Leigh Academy School in Tonbridge.

Social, fun & welcoming

Pickleball is still a relatively new sport in the UK, but we're learning it as fast as we can.

Lots of court time

We've got availability for up to 20 participants on Thursday nights, as we have access to four courts.

Competitive opportunities

We actively pursue matches with neighboring clubs and participate in competitions throughout the UK.

Meet new people & learn new skills

Our social Pickleball evenings aim to deliver a mix of fun games and drills.

Meet new people

Our group comprises individuals of all ages, sexes and walks of life - everyone is welcome.

Learn new skills

We're learning as we play Pickleball. We've yet to master the third shot drop, still get caught in the kitchen for volleys and play as if it were a game of mini tennis, but we're getting there!

Stay active & keep fit

Whilst Pickleball doubles offers a great workout, we occasionally switch it up and play singles for more vigorous heart-pumping Pickle!

Join us!

It's free to join. We currently play on Thursday evenings.
